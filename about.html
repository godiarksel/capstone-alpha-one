ANNUAL CONGRESS OF DISCOURSE
2022

The Congress brings together the most brilliant minds in modern Philosophy to share knowledge and discuss about the latest advancements in the numerous fields of philosophy. We have philosphy professors from all over the globe who grace us with their attendance to give passionate lectures on the translations of all our favorite ancient philosophers.

Mission

To keep the fire of philosophy burning and bring the greatest minds together in order to further the study of all matters concerning the enlightment of man.

Vision

To use our new-found hologram technology to reach more scholars and provide live lectures of all the ancient philosophers in more than 50 languages all thanks to the efforts of our translation teams.
